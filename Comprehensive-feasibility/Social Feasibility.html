Social Feasibility Analysis

A social feasibility study aims to assess the expected impact of the project on the local community in terms of job creation, infrastructure support, social responsibility enhancement, and the community's acceptance of the project. The purpose of this questionnaire is to identify positive impacts and potential risks, ensuring the project aligns with the needs and requirements of the local community. Please answer accurately and transparently based on the best available estimates, as this data will be used to form a comprehensive picture of the project's social feasibility.
